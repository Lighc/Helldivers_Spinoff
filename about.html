Warbonds

Warbonds are like battle passes but better. They don't expire, preventing FOMO, they can be unlocked without microtransactions. You get a free warbond when you start and can unlock more for 1000 Super Credits which can be bought or acquired from missions. Warbonds add new armor, weapons, stratagems, armor skins and boosters. Warbonds are themed like explosive firepower, fire, chemical weapons and more. Right now, there are also 2 premium warbonds for 1500 super credits which are collaborations with Halo and Killzone.

 

Stratagems

Stratagems are used to call in support weapons, sentries, reinforcements which revive your fallen friends and orbital bombardemnts. Basic stratagems can be unlocked with requisitions which are acquired from missions and even more can be gotten from warbonds. They are sorted into different categories.
